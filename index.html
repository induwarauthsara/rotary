Welcome to Rotary Institute of Higher Education

Welcome to the Rotary Institute of Higher Education. Popularly known as ‘the tuition ’ it is one of the most popular Sri Lankan Private Educational Institutes which is located in the heart of the city of Nugegoda, surrounded by leading schools and universities and approximately 5km to the Capital business city, Colombo, Sri Lanka. Rotary Institute of Higher Education is based within more than one acre with modern buildings and class room facilities. With over 40 years of excellence, it is regarded as the pioneer tuition institute and as one of the country’s leading Educational providers. Rotary Institute of Higher Education facilitator is a home to any age group student population in Sri Lankan Education field including Primary, Secondary and recruiting students to a multitude of universities and educational institutes in various countries.
